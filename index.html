Timeline of halo games involving Master Chief:

  • Halo: Combat Evolved - Master Chief first appears in the games as the protagonist of Halo: Combat Evolved.
  • Halo 2 - The Master Chief returns as one of two playable characters in Halo 2, the 2004 sequel to Halo: Combat Evolved.
  • Halo 3 - Back on Earth, the Master Chief helps to repel hostile Covenant forces.
  • Halo 4 - Master Chief awakens from the cryo-pod he entered at the end of Halo 3 within the Forward Unto Dawn.
  • Halo 5: Guardians - Blue Team finds a dormant Guardian, an autonomous Forerunner ship.
